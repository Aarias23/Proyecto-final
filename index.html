Capítulo IV: Lectura y Comprensión

Texto: John's Daily Routine

John's Daily Routine

John is a 25-year-old engineer who lives in New York City. Every day, he wakes up at 6:00 AM. First, he takes a shower and gets dressed. Then, he eats breakfast, usually eggs and toast, and drinks a cup of coffee. After breakfast, John leaves his apartment and walks to the subway station. He takes the subway to his office, which is located in downtown Manhattan.

John starts work at 8:00 AM. He spends most of his day in meetings and working on various engineering projects. For lunch, he often goes to a nearby café with his colleagues. They usually have sandwiches or salads. In the afternoon, John continues working until 5:00 PM.

After work, John likes to go to the gym. He exercises for about an hour before heading home. In the evening, he cooks dinner, watches some TV, and sometimes reads a book. John usually goes to bed around 10:00 PM, ready to start the next day.

Preguntas de Comprensión:

  1. How old is John?
  2. Where does John live?
  3. What time does John wake up every day?
  4. What does John eat for breakfast?
  5. How does John get to his office?
  6. Where is John's office located?
  7. What does John do for lunch?
  8. What time does John finish work?
  9. What does John do after work?
  10. What time does John usually go to bed?
