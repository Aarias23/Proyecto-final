Capítulo II: Estructuras Gramaticales y Vocabulario

Verbo To Be

  • 10 affirmative sentences using the verb "to be".
  • 10 negative sentences using the verb "to be".
  • 10 questions using the verb "to be".

Simple Present

  • 10 affirmative sentences in the simple present tense.
  • 10 negative sentences in the simple present tense.
  • 10 questions using the auxiliaries "Do" and "Does".

Present Continuous

  • 10 affirmative sentences in the present continuous tense.
  • 10 negative sentences in the present continuous tense.
  • 10 questions in the present continuous tense.

Wh- Questions

  • Write 7 questions using "Wh- Questions" (What, Where, When, Why, Who, How, Which).

Possessive Adjectives

  • Write 10 sentences using the following possessive adjectives: my, your, his, her, its, their, our.

Chapter IV: Reading and Comprehension

Text: Sophia's Daily Routine

Sophia is a 30-year-old graphic designer who lives in Los Angeles, California. She starts her day at 7:00 AM. The first thing she does is stretch and meditate for ten minutes. After that, she takes a shower and gets dressed. For breakfast, she usually eats yogurt with granola and a smoothie.

At 8:00 AM, Sophia begins her workday from home. She checks her emails and then starts working on design projects for her clients. She works until 1:00 PM, when she takes a lunch break. Around noon, she takes a break and goes for a short walk in the park near her apartment.

In the evening, Sophia enjoys doing yoga or going for a run at the beach. Around 7:30 PM, she makes dinner, often trying new recipes. After eating, she watches a TV series or listens to a podcast. Sometimes, she sketches or writes in her journal.

By 10:30 PM, she gets ready for bed, reads a few pages of a book, and then goes to sleep, ready for a new day.

Preguntas de Comprensión:

  1. How old is Sophia?
  2. Where does she live?
  3. What is the first thing Sophia does in the morning?
  4. What does she usually eat for breakfast?
  5. Where does she work?
  6. What does Sophia do around noon?
  7. What does she eat for lunch?
  8. What time does she finish working?
  9. What does Sophia do in the evening?
  10. What time does she go to bed?
