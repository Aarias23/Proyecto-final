Capítulo II: Estructuras Gramaticales y Vocabulario

Verbo To Be

  • 10 affirmative sentences using the verb "to be".
  • 10 negative sentences using the verb "to be".
  • 10 questions using the verb "to be" and answer each of them using short answer.

Simple Present

  • 10 affirmative sentences in the simple present tense.
  • 10 negative sentences in the simple present tense.
  • 10 questions using the auxiliaries "Do" and "Does".

Present Continuous

  • 10 affirmative sentences in the present continuous tense.
  • 10 negative sentences in the present continuous tense.
  • 10 questions in the present continuous tense.

Wh- Questions

  • Write 7 questions using "Wh- Questions" (What, Where, When, Why, Who, How, Which).

Possessive Adjectives

  • Write 10 sentences using the following possessive adjectives: my, your, his, her, its, their, our.

Chapter IV: Reading and Comprehension

Text: Ana the Teacher

Ana is a teacher in a public school. She wakes up at 6:00 AM every day. First, she eats breakfast with her family and then she takes the bus to school. Her classes start at 7:30 AM.

In the classroom, Ana teaches English to young students. She explains grammar, asks questions, and gives exercises. She always speaks slowly so her students can understand her. The students enjoy her classes because she is patient and friendly.

At noon, Ana eats lunch in the school cafeteria with other teachers. She usually eats rice, beans, and chicken. After lunch, she prepares lesson plans and checks students’ notebooks. She corrects their mistakes and writes feedback.

In the afternoon, Ana sometimes attends meetings with other teachers. She also talks with parents about their children’s progress. At 3:00 PM, she finishes her work and takes the bus back home.

At home, Ana spends time with her children. She helps them with homework, cooks dinner, and cleans the house. In the evening, she reads a book or listens to music. She usually goes to bed at 10:00 PM because she wakes up early every day.

Comprehension Questions:

  1. What is Ana’s job?
  2. What time does Ana wake up?
  3. How does she go to school?
  4. What subject does she teach?
  5. Why do students enjoy her classes?
  6. Where does Ana eat lunch?
  7. What does she do after lunch?
  8. Who does Ana talk to in the afternoon?
  9. What does Ana do at home in the evening?
  10. Why does Ana go to bed early?
